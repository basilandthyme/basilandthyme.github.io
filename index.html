About Me

You know those moments in life where you reach your fingers out to grasp something you’ve always wanted? But only to pull back last minute and let it graze pass you instead, because you knew it was too impossible, because you’ll never get hired without the experience, because you’re too old to learn, because you’re too young to try…

Like you, I’ve told myself those same millions of reasons to why “I can’t”. Even when I really wanted to.

Last year, I decided to turned every “if only” that came my way into a “yes!” and treaded my feet in drawing, designing, public speaking, marketing, radio broadcasting, a new language, business & even gymnastic.

Welcome to my ongoing learning portfolio—where I document the “Sure, I’m on it!” moments in life.
